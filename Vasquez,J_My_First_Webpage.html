4 of my Favorite Art Supplies

  1. Graphite Pencils

    2. Essential for sketching and shading, they offer precision and control for both detailed and rough drawings.

  2. Alcohol Markers

    3. Blend smoothly, dry fast, and layer well, making them great for vibrant illustrations and comic-style art.

  3. Watercolor Paints

    4. Create soft, flowing effects and beautiful gradients, perfect for expressive astmospheric artwork.

  4. Fine Liner Pens

    5. Ideal for outlining and adding many details, they prove crisps, smudge-proof lines for ink and design work.
